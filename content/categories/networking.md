Once installed, you can see that other users connected to the hotspot will appear automatically on the "connections" tab, with an Wi-Fi icon next to their names. You can start exchanging data with them by clicking on them and connecting or opening their profiles and following them!

However, to connect to other people outside of the local network, you'll need an invitation to an online SSB "pub" or "room". Conveniently, this hotspot already runs a pub that you can connect to: just paste the invitation below where it says "paste invitations" (also on the connections tab).

<invite></invite>

This pub is connected to some rooms out there on the Internet, so if you follow the pub itself you can see other users (in ssb, the pubs are a special kind of user - more on that on the <a href="http://scuttlebot.io/more/protocols/secure-scuttlebutt.html">documentation</a>).
