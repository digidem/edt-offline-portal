Would you like to store and share your files with your neighbors without giving them to corportations? Let's use the interplanetary, distributed and p2p filesystem, [IPFS](https://ipfs.io). Each file that is shared receives an unique identifierv that can be used to reach it from anyhwere in the Internet.

An instance of the IPFS server is running on your local server.
