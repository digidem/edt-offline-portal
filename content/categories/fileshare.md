You want to store and share files with your neighbors without giving them to the big corporations? This is the place. Check out <a href="http://p2p.local:5001/webui">IPFS</a>, a distributed, p2p, file system. Every file shared through IPFS receive an unique identifier that you can use to reach it in the whole network.
